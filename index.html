ॐ

Dear Punita Ma'am

I am very happy that you're my TEACHER and GURU,
I enjoy each lesson you teach,
As my role model you inspire me,
To dream and to work and to reach,

With your kindness you get my attention,
Everyday you plant a seed of curiosity and motivation,
To know and to go and succeed,

you help me fulfill my potential,
I am thankful for all that you have done,
I admire you each day,
I just want to say,
गुरुर्ब्रह्मा ग्रुरुर्विष्णुः गुरुर्देवो महेश्वरः ।
गुरुः साक्षात् परं ब्रह्म तस्मै श्री गुरवे नमः । ।

Aishwarya Dhoot(7th C)
